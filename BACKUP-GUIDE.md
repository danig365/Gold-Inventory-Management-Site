# Data Backup Guide
### New Jehlum Gold Smith Management System

---

## What Is This?

Your data is now automatically backed up every day. This means if anything ever goes wrong — accidental deletion, a mistake, or anything unexpected — you can bring your data back to how it was on any of the last 7 days.

---

## How to Open the Backup Panel

1. Log in to your account
2. Look at the **left sidebar menu**
3. Click **"Data Backup"** — it has a shield icon 🛡️

A panel will slide open from the right side of the screen.

---

## Understanding the Colored Dot

Next to the shield icon, you will see a small colored dot:

| Dot Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | Your data was backed up recently — you are safe |
| 🟡 Yellow | Backup is a bit old — consider creating one manually |
| 🔴 Red | No backup yet — create one manually right away |

---

## What You Can See in the Panel

Once you open the panel, you will see:

- **How many backups** you have
- **How many days** of history are covered
- **When the last backup was made** (e.g. "3 hours ago")
- A list of all your backups with their dates and times

---

## Creating a Backup Manually

You do not need to do this every day — it happens automatically. But if you want to save a backup right now (for example, before making big changes):

1. Open the **Data Backup** panel
2. Click the **"Create Backup Now"** button
3. Wait a moment — you will see a green message: *"Backup created successfully"*

Done. Your backup is saved.

---

## Restoring Your Data

If you want to go back to how your data looked on a previous day:

1. Open the **Data Backup** panel
2. Find the backup from the date you want in the list
3. Click the **"Restore"** button next to it
4. Read the confirmation message and click **"Restore Now"**
5. Your data will update immediately — you will see a green message confirming success

> **Important:** Before restoring, the system automatically saves a copy of your current data as a safety backup. So even after restoring, you can undo it if needed.

---

## How Often Are Backups Made?

- **Automatically:** Once every 24 hours (runs in the background, no action needed)
- **Manually:** Anytime you click "Create Backup Now"
- **How long kept:** The last **7 days** of backups are always kept. Older ones are removed automatically.

---

## Things to Know

- Each person's backups are **private** — only you can see and restore your own data
- You can always see **when** the last backup was made from the sidebar dot color
- Restoring always creates a safety copy first, so you can never lose data by mistake
